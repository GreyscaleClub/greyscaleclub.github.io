Greyscale

A Club @ The University of Victoria

The goal of this club is to provide club members with a safe and friendly learning environment in regards to ethical hacking and project prototyping with both software and hardware. We intend to provide project incubation and support for students who wish to undertake technical projects but lack resources and/or peer input.
